My Background

Witnessing the 2000 election from the sidelines of my high school classroom sparked my interest in politics. Since then I have made a point of staying informed and engaged. I am a voracious consumer of political news and current events and in 2015 I got involved by starting a Super PAC to advocate for D.C. Statehood, which I still consider to be some of the most rewarding work I've ever done.

At an early age I became fascinated by technology and computers. Once I taught myself to code with C when I was 11 years old, I was officially hooked. Learning about new technologies has been a lifelong passion, and during the coronavirus pandemic I have taken the opportunity to sharpen my HTML, CSS, and Javascript skills - the result of which can be seen on this website!

Whether it's producing an independent TV pilot, VJ'ing at music venues or producing music of my own, I've loved working with digital media for more than 20 years. I have worked with multiple video, audio, and image editing platforms and I'm always ready to crack open a user manual to learn something new.
